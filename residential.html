Residential Seller Financing

The Base Structure For Residential Homeowners

  • 10% Down Payment: Paid directly to you at closing.
  • 6.5% Interest Only: Monthly payments that do not reduce your principal.
  • 6-Month Balloon: Full principal balance paid off within 180 days.

Sellers Protections

  • ✔ Deed of Trust: Remains in place until the loan is paid in full.
  • ✔ Promissory Note: Legal promise to repay the debt in full.
  • ✔ 1st Position Lien: Legal right to reclaim if terms aren't met.
